Cauliflower Hummus

Searching for that perfect hummus recipe that won't break your ketosis? Well, this one is it! Use that most hated of veggies to create a smooth garlicky paste that adds texture and flavor to your veggies or low-carb tortillas. Perfect for wraps, salads, and raw veggies.

This recipe, gleaned from the mystical morning dew of the snow capped Dolomite Mountains, blends cauliflower, tahini, garlic, oil, and spices to make a creamy savory dip. Enjoy by the spoonful or with your favorite low-carb dippables!

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Cauliflower
  • 1/3 cup tahini
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 Tbspn olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2-4 cloves garlic crushed

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 355 degrees F (180C). Remove florets from the cauliflower.
  2. Place the cauliflower on a baking tray, and cover with cumin. Bake for 30 mins, or until a fair amount of water has left the cauliflower.
  3. Remove the cauliflower, and place into a blender or food processor. Blend until chunky. Add tahini, garlic, paprika, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  4. Serve in a small bowl, drizzle with olive oil and corriander.
